coTHERM Method

Time-correlated thermophysical property evaluation for accelerating detailed transport model (DTM) simulations in OpenFOAM.

Overview

coTHERM reduces computational cost in DTM by exploiting temporal correlation of thermodynamic states, avoiding full recomputation of properties at every time step.
